Chameleon

Some Interesting facts about chameleons :-

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin colour is an important part of communication among chameleons.
  • Most chameleons have a prehensive tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Pigeon

Some Interesting facts about pigeons :-

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  • Pigeons are reknowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Teddy-Bear

Some interesting facts about teddy-bears :-

  • Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot
    a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902.
  • The first toy stuffed bear was created by German toymaker Margarete Steiff.
    She created a soft plush with movable arms and legs and a friendly face after taking her nephew to the zoo in 1903.
  • A Teddy bear helps to stimulate and develop people's imagination.

Panda

Some interesting facts about pandas :-

  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  • Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown).
  • Pandas have carnivorous teeth, but they eat bamboo and fruit.

Camel

Some interesting facts about camels :-

  • Camels Don't Store Water in Their Humps.
  • They Can Hydrate Quickly.
  • Camels Are Social Animals.

Flying-cicada

Some interesting facts about flying-cicadas :-

  • Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs.
  • Flying-cicadas are true bugs (from the order Hemiptera).
  • Females may be attracted to the sound of motors.
